# SEDA Industrial Energy Audit Checklist (V4)

*Source: SEDA Malaysia – EACG Scheme.  Industrial facilities.*

---

## Preamble — General Requirements

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Reporting template** | REA shall follow the latest reporting template |
| **Format** | Use correct SI units (e.g. °C, kWh, kW, m²) with a space between number and unit. Tables and figures must be clear, labeled, numbered, and referenced in the text. Table titles and figure captions should briefly explain the content of each table or figure. Images and charts must be high resolution and easy to read. Appendices must be labeled (e.g. Appendix A) and referenced in the report with a brief explanation. |
